About

The Man Behind
the Crates

Michael Strain — aka DJ Moodswing — is a vinyl purist with an omnivorous ear. Born from a love of hip hop and raised on a diet of soul, disco, jazz, and everything in between, Moodswing has built a reputation for reading the room and taking it somewhere unexpected.

Every set is curated from his ever-growing collection of wax. No playlists, no algorithms — just two turntables, a mixer, and decades of crate-digging paying off in real time.

Whether it's a house party, a rooftop, a wedding, or a club night, DJ Moodswing brings energy, knowledge, and the kind of musical versatility that keeps every crowd on their feet — and coming back for more.
